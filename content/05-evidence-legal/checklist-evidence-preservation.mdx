# Evidence Preservation Checklist

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`checklist`  ·  Level 1  ·  evidence · preservation · hashing · screenshots · jurisdiction</span>

## Core idea
Evidence must be preserved so it holds up when handed to law enforcement or lawyers. Get the basics right consistently.

## Components
- Hash files and keep a proper record of them.
- Capture full screenshots, including the timestamp and full context, not cropped fragments.
- Understand the legal framework and jurisdiction you operate in, which differs by country.
- Understand the downstream impact of your report before handing it over.

## When to use
Throughout a case, every time you capture or store a piece of evidence, and before any handover.
