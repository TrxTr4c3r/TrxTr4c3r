# Pre-Investigation Readiness Checklist

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`checklist`  ·  Level 1  ·  readiness · checklist · investigations · opsec · evidence-preservation</span>

## Core idea
A seven-item checklist to confirm before starting an investigation, covering scope, OPSEC, legality, evidence handling, and bias.

## Components
- I have a clear, documented research question and defined scope.
- I have a dedicated investigator browser profile with no personal accounts signed in.
- I am using a VPN or Tor for queries related to this investigation.
- I have an evidence preservation workflow in place.
- I understand the legal framework in my jurisdiction for this type of data collection.
- I have considered the potential impact of my findings on named individuals.
- I will actively seek disconfirming evidence, not just evidence supporting my hypothesis.

## When to use
Immediately before commencing collection on any new investigation.

## Example
Confirming a VPN is active and no personal accounts are signed into the investigator browser profile before running the first query on a suspect wallet.
