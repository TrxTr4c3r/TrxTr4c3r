# Investigator OPSEC Checklist

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`checklist`  ·  Level 1  ·  opsec · operational-security · osint · safety · puppet-accounts</span>

## Core idea
When investigating, you leave digital footprints too. These measures separate you personally from the investigation and stop a target from spotting you.

## Components
- Use a VPN at all times. Treat it as essential against attempts to reveal your IP, such as malicious links.
- Be wary of Tor; use it only rarely.
- Never view a target's social media with your personal profile. Platform algorithms (for example Facebook) can surface you to the target as a suggested connection.
- Use puppet accounts that look like ordinary people, with no link back to you, for social-media research.
- Stay pseudonymous publicly, but reveal your identity when working under contract with clients, lawyers, or law enforcement.

## When to use
Before and during any OSINT work that touches social platforms or a target's online presence.
