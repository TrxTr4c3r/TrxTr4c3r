# DeFi Layering

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`</span>

## Core idea
Routes funds through multiple swaps, lending pools, or liquidity pools across several protocols in quick succession purely to add hops, not for genuine investment.

## When to use
Multiple protocol interactions in a short window with no holding period, immediately followed by withdrawal to a fresh wallet.

## Example
Funds cycle through a DEX, a lending pool, and a liquidity pool within minutes, then exit to a new wallet, adding hops with no economic purpose.
