# Chain Hopping / Cross-Chain Bridging

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · chain-hopping · bridges · cross-chain · typology</span>

## Core idea
Moves funds across blockchains via bridges, atomic swaps, or DEX aggregators specifically to exit the coverage of whichever analytics tool is tracing the case.

## When to use
Funds vanish on one chain and reappear at matching value/timing on another; treat any bridge hop as a possible tracing break point.

## Example
Bitcoin is bridged to a wrapped asset on another chain and swapped, breaking the single-chain trail an analytics tool was following.
