# Nested Services / Omnibus Wallets

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · nested-services · omnibus-wallet · exchanges · typology</span>

## Core idea
Deposits into a smaller, often-unregistered platform that itself holds customer funds inside one pooled wallet at a larger exchange, hiding individual activity behind the larger exchange's address.

## When to use
A 'wallet' that turns out to be an exchange's own hot wallet servicing thousands of unrelated users.

## Avoid when
Don't assume the address owner is the suspect; an omnibus or hot wallet aggregates thousands of unrelated users behind one address.

## Example
Tracing ends at what looks like a single wallet, but it is a large exchange's pooled hot wallet used by a nested service's many customers.
