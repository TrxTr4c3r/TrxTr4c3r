# Privacy Coin Conversion

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`</span>

## Core idea
Converts traceable crypto into a privacy coin such as Monero to sever the visible trail, then converts back to a traceable asset later once scrutiny has passed.

## When to use
The swap into a privacy coin is itself the flag; treat funds re-emerging afterward as unlinkable without off-chain corroboration.

## Example
Stolen funds are swapped to Monero, held, then later swapped back to a stablecoin on a different exchange with no visible on-chain link between the two legs.
