# Peel Chain

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · peel-chain · layering · ransomware · typology</span>

## Core idea
Sends a large balance through a sequence of wallets, peeling off a small amount at each hop while the bulk continues forward, layering the trail with hundreds of micro-transactions. Common after ransomware, hacks, and darknet sales.

## When to use
A single large inflow followed by a long chain of decreasing, sequential single-output sends.

## Example
A hacked balance moves hop to hop, dropping a small amount to exchanges each step while the remainder rolls to a fresh wallet, building a chain of hundreds of micro-transactions.
