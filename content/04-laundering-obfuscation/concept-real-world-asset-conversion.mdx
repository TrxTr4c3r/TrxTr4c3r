# Real-World Asset Conversion

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · real-world-assets · gift-cards · luxury-goods · typology</span>

## Core idea
Spends illicit crypto directly on gift cards, luxury goods, or real estate, then resells or leverages those assets for clean fiat.

## When to use
Point-of-sale or marketplace records show unusually large or repeated crypto payments, and the on-chain trail ends at the vendor.

## Example
Illicit stablecoins buy luxury watches from a crypto-accepting dealer; the watches are resold for clean cash and the chain trail dead-ends at the vendor.
