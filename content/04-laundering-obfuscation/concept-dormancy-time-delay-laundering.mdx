# Dormancy / Time-Delay Laundering

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · dormancy · time-delay · cold-storage · typology</span>

## Core idea
Holds stolen funds untouched for months or years before moving them, betting that investigative attention and evidentiary windows fade over time.

## When to use
A wallet with one large inflow long ago and no activity since; still flag and monitor it rather than deprioritising.

## Example
Coins from a years-old hack suddenly move after a long dormancy, once the actor assumes scrutiny has faded.
