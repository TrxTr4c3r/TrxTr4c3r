# Structuring / Smurfing

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · structuring · smurfing · threshold-evasion · typology</span>

## Core idea
Breaks a large sum into many small transactions to stay under reporting or monitoring thresholds instead of moving it in one flagged transfer.

## When to use
Watch for repeated, near-identical, sub-threshold amounts from the same cluster within a short window.

## Example
Dozens of transfers of just under a reporting threshold leave one cluster within an hour, each to a different deposit address.
