# Wash Trading (incl. NFTs)

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`</span>

## Core idea
Repeatedly buys and sells the same asset, often via linked wallets, to fabricate trading volume or dress illicit proceeds up as legitimate trading gains.

## When to use
A small, closed set of wallets trading the same asset back and forth with no net economic change.

## Example
Two linked wallets trade the same NFT at rising prices to manufacture a legitimate-looking capital gain.
