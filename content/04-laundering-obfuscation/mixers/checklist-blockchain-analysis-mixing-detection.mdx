# How Blockchain Analysis Detects Mixing Activity

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`checklist`  ·  Level reference  ·  blockchain-forensics · detection · clustering · flow-tracing · risk-scoring</span>

## Core idea
The analytical methods used to identify and unwind mixing activity. Together they convert an apparently broken trail into linkable evidence.

## Components
- Identify interactions with known mixer or tumbler addresses
- Trace fund flows through complex transaction patterns
- Detect clustering of addresses and linkage behavior
- Analyze timing, amounts, inputs, outputs, and change addresses
- Track cross-chain movement and asset conversions
- Leverage machine learning and risk-scoring models
- Support investigations, SARs, and regulatory reporting

## When to use
Use when planning a tracing approach against a mixed flow, or when documenting the methodology behind a finding.
