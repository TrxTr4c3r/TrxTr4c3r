# OTC Brokering / Underground Guarantee Markets

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`</span>

## Core idea
Routes funds through informal P2P brokers or Telegram-based 'guarantee' marketplaces that consolidate many small criminal deposits into large stablecoin blocks for cash-out, often within minutes.

## When to use
Rapid, high-value stablecoin transfers to wallets with no retail trading history, tied to known broker clusters.

## Example
In 2025, Chinese-language underground networks alone were estimated to process roughly $16.1B via OTC brokers and guarantee markets that clear large stablecoin transfers in minutes.
