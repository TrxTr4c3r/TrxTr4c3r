# Gambling Platform Laundering

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`concept`  ·  Level reference  ·  money-laundering · gambling · casino · clean-narrative · typology</span>

## Core idea
Deposits illicit funds into an online crypto casino, places minimal-risk bets, then withdraws the balance as 'winnings' to manufacture a clean source-of-funds narrative.

## When to use
Deposit and withdrawal amounts nearly identical with minimal betting activity in between.

## Example
Funds are deposited to a crypto casino, a handful of tiny bets are placed, then nearly the same amount is withdrawn and reported as gambling winnings.
