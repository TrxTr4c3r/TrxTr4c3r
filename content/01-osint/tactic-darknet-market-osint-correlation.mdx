# Darknet Market & OSINT Correlation

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`tactic`  ·  Level reference  ·  blockchain-forensics · osint · darknet · attribution</span>

## Core idea
Cross-references on-chain clusters with open-source intelligence -- forum posts, vendor listings, or leaked databases -- to tie an address to a darknet-marketplace persona.

## When to use
Investigating narcotics, stolen-data, or contraband sales where the suspect uses a pseudonymous marketplace identity.

## Avoid when
OSINT sourcing must be lawfully obtained and documented, and the authenticity of forum data may need further corroboration.

## Example
A vendor's reused withdrawal address posted in a forum signature links their darknet persona to an on-chain cluster.
