# View a Wallet's Exposure with impersonator.xyz

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`tactic`  ·  Level 2  ·  osint · tooling · approvals · victim-exposure · impersonator · drainer-prevention</span>

## Core idea
Tools like impersonator.xyz let you view a wallet's own perspective on-chain -- what tokens, approvals, and signatures it has been exposed to from someone else's vantage point. This surfaces information hidden in the normal explorer view.

## Components
- Reveals stale approvals waiting to be drained.
- Reveals signed but unbroadcast transactions.
- Surfaces other latent risk indicators from the wallet's own viewpoint.

## When to use
Both to pre-empt drains (spotting live risk before it is exploited) and to reconstruct what a victim was actually exposed to during an investigation.

## Example
Viewing a compromised wallet through impersonator.xyz shows a stale unlimited approval and a signed-but-unbroadcast transaction that the normal explorer view hides -- explaining the pending drain vector and guiding remediation.
