# Exchange / VASP KYC Correlation

<span style={{ fontFamily: 'var(--font-space-mono), monospace', fontSize: 13, opacity: 0.7 }}>`tactic`  ·  Level reference  ·  blockchain-forensics · kyc · vasp · attribution · legal-process</span>

## Core idea
Matches an on-chain cluster's deposit or withdrawal address to a Virtual Asset Service Provider, then uses legal process to obtain KYC records tying the cluster to a real identity. Often the step that unmasks a suspect.

## When to use
Once clustering narrows the investigation to a specific exchange deposit address.

## Avoid when
Requires proper legal process (subpoena, warrant, or MLAT for foreign VASPs), and data retention varies by jurisdiction, so act before records age out.

## Example
A cluster's withdrawals all land at one exchange deposit address; a subpoena (or MLAT for a foreign exchange) returns the KYC identity behind it.
